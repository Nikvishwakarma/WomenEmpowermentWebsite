WE VALUE TRANSPARENCY

At Women’s Empowerment International, we want you to have total confidence that your donation is being used wisely and effectively. That’s why we work so hard to identify high-impact partners in the places where poverty is most entrenched and services are woefully inadequate. We offer professional management and track every dollar, to ensure both the quality and impact of our work. You can trust that your charitable investment goes further to empower the very neediest and hardest-working women. It’s a proven model you can trust.

THERE IS NO FUTURE WITHOUT EDUCATION

We envision a world without poverty in which women are empowered, uplifted and equal partners in society. Women live in safe, sustainable environments that include adequate food, water, housing, education and health care. They possess the tools to provide a better life for themselves and their families and they have the hope, opportunity and ability to control their lives and futures. Education is the key that can remove darkness from our life and make our life easy and happy.

WE GOAL OF FAMALE EDUCATION MUST INVARIABLY

Our mission is to empower women with tools to work their way out of poverty, care for their families, and strengthen their communities. We envision a world without poverty in which women are empowered, uplifted, and equal partners in society. We fund economic empowerment programs, including microfinance programs, small business development support, training, education, and other holistic poverty alleviation strategies, benefitting thousands of women experiencing poverty around the world.
